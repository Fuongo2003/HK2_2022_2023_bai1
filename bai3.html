One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begin re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes accquainted with the inner city streets, and realize the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Ghisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
